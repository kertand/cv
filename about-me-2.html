Hello!

I’m James Harrison, a visionary and co-founder of Hektor, focused on turning bold ideas into clear strategy and direction. I enjoy looking at the bigger picture and making sure every project moves with purpose and supports real business goals.

With a background in digital strategy and brand development, James works closely with clients to uncover opportunities and shape ideas into meaningful solutions. He believes that strong design starts with clear thinking and the courage to challenge the obvious.

At the core of his work is a simple goal: helping brands grow with intention. By combining strategic insight with creative perspective, James helps teams move forward with confidence and clarity.
